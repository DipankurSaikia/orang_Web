Facilities of Orang Hostel

Here is a list of facilities that are provided in Orang Hostel:

SIS Security

The SIS provide security over the students 24x7 and are always ready for help

Mess

You will get healthy and heigenic food with staff member always ready to serve you

Badminton Court

A badminton court is made in the middle of the hostel so students can enjoy their time playing the sport

Electricity

Electricity is provided 24x7 with no power cuts

Water Supply

Water is always available for use. Filtered drinking water are provided at each floors along with hot waters is provided in winter days for bathing

WIFI

Every floor has its own Wifi router with fast downloading and uploading speeds
